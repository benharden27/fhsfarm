Food Justice for All

Food Justice means making sure that everyone can grow, access and eat nutritious, sustainable and cultutrally-appropriate foods. At FHS we grow food to feed ourselves and our communiuty and work with experts throughout the region to understand how to make a difference.

Classes in the Pathway

Take the following classes at FHS to complete the pathway and earn industry recognized credentials

Level 1: Intro to Growing Food

Learn how to grow food and how it can help us solve challenges in our society

Level 2: Farming For Social Change

Level-up your skills in a larger farm site and work with community members to act on Food Justice projects

Level 3: School Farm Management

Take control of the farm to learn about crop planning and food distribution for a fairer future.
